About FRC Learnings

FRC Learnings is an independent training initiative focused on practical AI, GenAI, Agentic AI, and GCP DevOps skills for software professionals and engineering graduates.

Our Mission

Technology is changing faster than ever. Traditional tech stacks like classic ASP.NET and older web frameworks are quickly being supplemented or replaced by AI-powered workflows. FRC Learnings exists to help engineers transition into this new era.

We design batches that simulate real work scenarios—integrating LLMs, building RAG systems, designing AI agents, and deploying solutions on cloud platforms like GCP.

Who We Train

  • Working professionals looking to switch or upgrade to AI roles
  • Fresh engineering graduates who want a strong, industry-ready foundation
  • Developers with .NET, Java, Node, or Angular experience
  • Teams who need customized corporate AI workshops

What Makes Us Different?

  • ✅ Live instructor-led sessions (no random recorded content)
  • ✅ Focus on hands-on implementation, not just theory
  • ✅ Real projects inspired by banking, fintech, and enterprise use-cases
  • ✅ Accessible for both freshers and experienced devs

FRC Learnings runs under a neutral training identity. The focus stays on skills, outcomes, and community—not on personal branding.
