Course Catalog

All tracks are designed to be practical, implementation-oriented, and focused on real project outcomes.

AI Fundamentals for Software Engineers

Beginner • 4 weeks

Ideal for fresh graduates and professionals from .NET, Java, Node, or other backgrounds who want to step into AI without heavy maths.

You will learn:

  • AI vs ML vs Deep Learning vs GenAI
  • Large Language Models (LLMs) and transformers
  • Prompt engineering basics & structured prompting
  • Using AI tools for coding, debugging, and documentation

Applied GenAI & RAG Bootcamp

Intermediate • 6–8 weeks

A hands-on program focused on building production-like GenAI applications and integrating them into software products.

You will learn:

  • RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) patterns
  • Vector databases and embeddings
  • LangChain or similar frameworks for orchestration
  • Building a domain-specific chatbot / knowledge assistant

Agentic AI & Workflow Automation

Advanced • 4–6 weeks

Learn to design AI agents that can plan, reason, and perform multi-step tasks using real APIs and systems.

You will learn:

  • Agentic AI patterns and multi-step reasoning
  • Task routing, tools, and action execution
  • Building agents for support, analytics, and workflows
  • Integrating agents with existing applications

GCP DevOps for AI Engineers

Professional Track • 4–6 weeks

Designed for engineers who want to deploy AI/GenAI services in a reliable, secure, and scalable way on Google Cloud Platform.

You will learn:

  • Containerization and deployment (Cloud Run, GKE basics)
  • Cloud SQL, GCS, IAM, KMS for AI workloads
  • CI/CD pipelines and configuration management
  • Monitoring, logging, and reliability best practices
Note: For the first month, selected tracks will be offered as free live batches to build the learning community and gather feedback. Seats are limited.
